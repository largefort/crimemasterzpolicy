Data Deletion Policy for CrimeMasterz

Effective Date: February 13, 2025

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of data when you create an account and play CrimeMasterz:

  • Account Information: Username and password.
  • Game Data: Progress, achievements, and in-game activities.
  • Device and Usage Information: IP address, device type, and usage patterns.

Please note that the game does not require an email address during account creation. Players create an account using only a username and password.

2. How to Request Data Deletion

As the game creator, you can manually reset or delete player accounts upon request. If you wish to have your game data deleted, you can contact us as follows:

  • Email Request: Send an email to largefort@gmail.com with the subject line "Request for Data Deletion." Please include your username associated with the account.

Once we receive your request, we will process it within 30 days. This includes a manual search of your username to reset or delete your game data.

3. Data Deletion Process

Upon receiving your request, we will proceed with deleting or resetting your game data manually:

  • Account Reset: If requested, your in-game progress, achievements, and other data will be reset, but your username will remain valid for future use.
  • Complete Data Deletion: If you request complete data deletion, all data associated with your account will be permanently erased, and you will lose access to any game progress, in-game purchases, and achievements.

4. Retention of Data

Certain non-personally identifiable data may be retained for legal, security, or backup purposes, as required by law.

5. Third-Party Services

CrimeMasterz may share your data with third-party services, such as analytics providers, to improve the game experience. If you request data deletion, we will also ensure third-party services handle your data in accordance with our deletion policy.

6. Children's Privacy

CrimeMasterz is rated for players aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal data from children under the age of 16. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under 16, we will promptly delete such information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Data Deletion Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated policy will take effect immediately upon posting.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Data Deletion Policy or if you need assistance with deleting or resetting your data, please contact us at:

  • Email: largefort@gmail.com
  • Website: https://crimemasterz.com
